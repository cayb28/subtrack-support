Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a subscription?

Tap the + button on the Subscriptions tab, search for the service (35+ recognized automatically), choose a plan, and confirm.

How do I delete a subscription?

Swipe left on any subscription card and tap delete. You can undo this action for a few seconds after.

How is the Health Score calculated?

It's based on 5 weighted criteria: budget ratio, usage rate, sharing optimization, renewal tracking, and annual vs monthly pricing.

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings → Language and choose between English, French, Spanish, or German.

Is my data private?

Yes. SubTrack stores everything locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

How does family sharing work?

When adding or editing a subscription, set the number of people sharing it. SubTrack automatically calculates your real per-person cost.
