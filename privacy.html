Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🔒 SubTrack stores all your data locally on your device. We do not operate any servers, collect any personal data, or share information with third parties.

Overview

SubTrack ("the App") is a subscription tracking application developed by Theo Dehas. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

Data Collection

SubTrack does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

All information you enter into the App — including subscription names, prices, renewal dates, usage ratings, and budget settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it yourself.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't collect your name, email, or any identifying information.
  • We don't track your location.
  • We don't use analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We don't share data with advertisers or third parties.
  • We don't have servers that store your subscription data.

Third-Party Services

SubTrack does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App may use your device's default email client if you choose to contact support, but this action is initiated entirely by you.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by removing individual subscriptions within the App, or by uninstalling the App entirely, which removes all associated local data.

Children's Privacy

SubTrack does not knowingly collect any data from children, as it does not collect any personal data at all. The App is rated 4+ and contains no content unsuitable for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
